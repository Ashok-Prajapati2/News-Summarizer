Domestic box office hits record Rs 1,291 crore in August 2024, led by Stree 2 and Independence Day releases, reveals Ormax report

The cumulative box office for Jan-Aug releases stands at Rs 6,868 crore which is 7.5% below the same period in 2023.

Written by BrandWagon Online
September 16, 2024 13:30 IST

The domestic box office collected Rs 1291 crore in the month of August, reveals Ormax report. From what is understood, this has been the highest collection of this year. The cumulative box office for Jan-Aug releases stands at Rs 6,868 crore which is 7.5% below the same period in 2023.

Stree 2 reported a collection of Rs 680 crores in the month of August with Saripodhaa Sanivaaram ranking second with a collection of Rs 77 crores. Furthermore, when the top 10 releases of Jan-Aug were taken into consideration, Kalki 2898AD became the highest grossing film with a box office collection of Rs 774 crores and Stree 2 came second with Rs 680 crores.
