National Engineer’s Day 2024: Python programming, Gen AI among in-demand skilling courses for engineers

National Engineering Day 2024: The most popular GenAI courses in India in the past 12 months include the introduction to Generative AI, generative AI for everyone, prompt engineering for ChatGT and Google AI essentials.

National Engineering Day 2024: Call it a thirst for learning or a poor economic situation, engineers are constantly seeking to upgrade their skills and stay competitive. Most technical institutes have recently started courses on data analysis and artificial intelligence. However, online platforms have provided short or degree courses for freshers and professionals for a decade. A survey by the ed-tech platform Great Learning suggests 89 per cent plan to acquire hard skills this year, with AI/ML being the most sought-after area, among which 86 per cent expressed a demand for upskilling in Generative AI.

Read |

AI skills are becoming increasingly important for future engineers, Anurag Gupta, co-founder of STEMROBO, a coding-based EdTech Company said. As industries move toward automation, data analysis, and innovative technologies, the need for engineers who understand AI is growing fast. In fact, by 2030, nearly 1 million AI-related jobs are expected to be available in India alone, the co-founder added saying that to meet this demand,  it is important to start learning AI and STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) from an early age. This allows students to develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills while gaining hands-on experience with new technologies. Students with early exposure to AI STEM education at a young age are more likely to pursue advanced degrees in these fields and enter STEM-related careers.

As per the online learning platforms including Coursera and Udemy, the most in-demand skills for engineers include GenAI courses, Python programming, algorithms, artificial neural networks, data analysis, Excel, presentation leadership, SQL (Structured Query Language), and cybersecurity.
