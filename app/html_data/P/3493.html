About P&G

Procter & Gamble Hygiene & Healthcare Ltd., incorporated in the year 1964, is a Large Cap company (having a market cap of Rs 53,629.19 Crore) operating in FMCG sector. Procter & Gamble Hygiene & Healthcare Ltd. key Products/Revenue Segments include Hygiene Products, Creams & Ointments, Cough Drops, Tablets for the year ending 30-Jun-2023. For the quarter ended 30-06-2024, the company has reported a Standalone Total Income of Rs 939.06 Crore, down 7.55 % from last quarter Total Income of Rs 1,015.76 Crore and up 8.86 % from last year same quarter Total Income of Rs 862.63 Crore. Company has reported net profit after tax of Rs 81.06 Crore in latest quarter. The companys top management includes Mr.Chittranjan Dua, Mr.L V Vaidyanathan, Mr.Ghanashyam Hegde, Mr.Gagan Sawhney, Mr.Karthik Natarajan, Mr.Pramod Agarwal, Ms.Sonali Dhawan, Mr.Anil Kumar Gupta, Mr.Gurcharan Das, Mr.Krishnamurthy Iyer, Ms.Meena Ganesh, Mr.Prashant Bhatnagar, Mr.Ghanashyam Hegde.
