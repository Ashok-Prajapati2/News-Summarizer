9 signs you have too much vitamin D

ET Online

Sep 21, 2024

Nausea

Excess Vitamin D can cause digestive discomfort, leading to nausea or vomiting.

Image Source: iStock

Loss of Appetite

Overdosing on Vitamin D may reduce your desire to eat, leading to unintended weight loss.

Image Source: iStock

Fatigue

High levels of Vitamin D can result in muscle weakness and feeling tired all the time.

Image Source: iStock

Frequent Urination

Too much Vitamin D can lead to excessive calcium, causing increased urination.

Image Source: iStock

Kidney Stones

High calcium levels from excess Vitamin D may cause the formation of kidney stones.

Image Source: iStock

Cognitive Issues

Over time, too much Vitamin D can impair cognitive function, causing confusion or disorientation.

Image Source: iStock

Dehydration

Vitamin D toxicity can lead to fluid imbalances, causing persistent thirst and dehydration.

Image Source: iStock

High Blood Pressure

Too much Vitamin D can elevate calcium levels, contributing to hypertension (high blood pressure).

Image Source: iStock

Bone Pain

Paradoxically, excess Vitamin D can weaken bones, leading to bone pain despite its role in calcium regulation.

Image Source: iStock
