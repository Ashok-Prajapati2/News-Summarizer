D Link India Company profile

ABOUT D Link India

  • Industry Communications Equipment
  • ISIN INE250K01012
  • BSE Code 533146
  • NSE Code DLINKINDIA

D-Link (India) Limited is an India-based company. The Company operates through a distribution network with a wide range of product portfolio and solutions with a nationwide reach across India. The principal business of the Company is marketing and distribution of D-Link branded Networking products. The Company offers wide range of products, such as Home Wi-Fi, Range Extenders, and Unmanaged Switches. Its Home Wireless range features cutting-edge connectivity solutions, prominently highlighted by the EAGLE PRO series that combines Wi-Fi with artificial intelligence (AI) offering advanced AI-based optimization for seamless high-speed Wi-Fi coverage. The Company’s Range Extenders are designed to eliminate Wi-Fi dead zones and improve coverage throughout home or office. Its Unmanaged Switches provide solution for expanding network connectivity. They offer various port options, including Gigabit, and Fast Ethernet. The Company's subsidiary is TeamF1 Networks Private Limited.
